 Stay Safe Online – Checklist for Students
•	Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone, no matter who they say they are 
•	Don’t approve “collect requests” unless you are completely sure you’re the one making a payment 
•	Always double-check QR codes before scanning, since fraudsters often send fake ones 
•	Don’t click on links that look unfamiliar or suspicious, whether they arrive via SMS, email, or WhatsApp 
•	Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) for all accounts that matter to you 
•	Use a strong, different password for each platform you use 
•	Keep your apps and phone updated so you always have the latest security patches 
•	Stay off public Wi-Fi when doing anything related to money or banking 

UPI / Financial Safety Tips (Important)
•	Remember: You do NOT need to enter a UPI PIN to receive money 
•	Immediately block your bank account and report if you notice any suspicious transaction
