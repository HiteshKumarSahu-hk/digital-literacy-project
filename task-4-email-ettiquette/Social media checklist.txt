Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist
 Do’s
•	Think before you post — ensure your content is respectful and appropriate 
•	Use strong privacy settings to protect your personal information 
•	Share verified and accurate information only 
•	Maintain a professional and positive tone on public platforms 
•	Engage respectfully with others and support healthy discussions 
Don’ts
•	Do not share sensitive personal details (address, phone number, passwords) 
•	Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content 
•	Do not spread fake news or unverified information 
•	Avoid engaging in online arguments or cyberbullying 
•	Don’t post anything that could harm your academic or professional reputation
